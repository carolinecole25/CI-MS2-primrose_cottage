Porthleven - Cornwall, South Coast

Porthleven

The harbour is fast gaining a reputation as the next Cornish hotspot when it comes to food tourism.

In the summer months when the tide is it is full of groups diving in from the sides, paddleboarders, and other watersports.

Walks

Coastal path goes both ways out of Porthleven.

Walking past The Ship Inn goes to Rinsey which is a 5km walk or to Praa Sands for a 10km walk.

Walking past the Atlantic Inn, there is a walk through the penrose Estate to Loe Bar.

Warning: you cannot swim in Loe Bar, it is very dangerous.
